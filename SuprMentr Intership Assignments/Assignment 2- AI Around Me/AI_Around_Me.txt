### 
AI Around Me – 10 Real-Life AI Applications I Use Daily

1. Google Search
   When I search something, AI ranks the best results using ML models that understand keywords, user behavior, and relevance.

2. YouTube Recommendations
   YouTube suggests videos based on what I watch, like, or skip using machine learning to predict my interests.

3. Instagram Feed & Reels
   Instagram shows posts and reels based on my activity (likes, shares, watch time) using ML recommendation systems.

4. Google Maps Navigation
   Maps predicts traffic and suggests fastest routes by analyzing real-time data using machine learning algorithms.

5. Voice Assistant (Google Assistant)
   It understands my voice commands using speech recognition and NLP (Natural Language Processing).

6. Spam Detection in Gmail
   Emails are automatically classified as spam or not using ML models trained on millions of email patterns.

7. Online Shopping (Amazon/Flipkart)
   These apps recommend products based on my browsing and purchase history using recommendation algorithms.

8. Auto-Correct & Predictive Text (Keyboard)
   My phone suggests words while typing using ML models trained on language patterns.

9. Face Unlock in Smartphone
   My phone unlocks by recognizing my face using computer vision and deep learning models.

10. Music Apps (Spotify/Gaana)
    These apps recommend songs based on my listening habits using ML to understand my music taste.
